Provisional Programme

Monday
  • Surveys and Statistics
    • Galactic Distribution, Kinematics, High-velocity stars, H-deficient stars in other galaxies
  • High Mass Stars
    • Wolf-Rayet stars, stripped stars
  • Binaries
    • AM CVn stars, Ups Sgr binaries
Tuesday
  • Low Mass Stars
    • R CrB stars, H-deficient giants, extreme helium stars, [WC] stars, O(He) stars, PG 1159 stars
    • He-sdB and He-sdO stars,DO, DB and DQ white dwarfs, model atmospheres
Wednesday
  • Transients
    • H-deficient novae, born-again stars, supernovae Ib/c
  • Conference Excursion
Thursday
  • Connections I: H-deficient stars from birth to death
    • Double white dwarf mergers, late-hot-flash and final-flash stars
  • Ejecta
    • R CrB shells, H-deficient dust, H-deficient chemistry
Friday
  • Connections II: H-deficient stars from birth to death
    • SN Ib/c progenitors, subdwarf binaries
  • Pulsation

The programme will also contain discussion sessions and poster sparkler sessions.
