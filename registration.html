Poster Printing Service

A poster printing service will be offered. Posters will be of A0 size, glossy finish and portrait orientation. The cost will be approximately £15 and is payable at registration. In order to get your poster printed we will need to receive it by 2pm on Wednesday 5th September. Details on how to submit posters for printing will be added here at a later date.
